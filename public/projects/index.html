TAP & APD

TAP & APD is an e-commerce website for managing and selling tortellini. There are 2 types of service users:

  1. Providers: CRUD operations for products and manage user account from a restricted dashboard;
  2. Consumers: can register, log in, and manage their cart.

Technology:

  • Laravel
  • MySQL
  • WSDL REST

Repo: Github
